Who We Are

Wimrey's Stock App is a global application that focuses on stock update.It one of the best application for updating stock in supermarkets not only in Kenya but also around the world.Wimrey Stock application is a business oriented service that ensures that businesses run smoothly without much difficulties. Our mission as an organisation is to ensure quality services to our clients across borders. Our vision is to grow deep in order to reach wide in terms of maintaining a good customer relations with all our clients and to ensure that their needs are all met when it comes to updating products.



Services Offered

Wimrey Stock is an application that targets mainly supermarkets since that is where most people do their shopping regularly.This application is dedicated in providing nothing but the best to its customers. Wimrey Stock Update offers various services to its customers and the main service is to provide a platform for the users to be able to list their products varieties and quantity respectively and by doing that the application is able to provide the users with an alert that he or she needs to restock a specific product in the shop when the quantity of the product reduces at a certain level.



Market Impact

This application has various impact on the market since it ensures continuity of goods and services supply across borders and this in turn, ensures economic stability in different nations. The buyers at the supermarkets will also benefit buyers at the supermarkets in terms of time.The buyers will save time when they go shopping and not running risks of finding that some of the products are missing on shelves and they will also get quality products since they will not run risks of purchasing expired products.
